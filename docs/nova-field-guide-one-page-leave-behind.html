Nova Field Guide

Mobile documentation support for appropriate-use discipline

Partner demo prototype

What it is

A phone-friendly field workflow designed to help qualified healthcare professionals document ocular surface findings, prior measures, treatment rationale, photos, notes, and follow-up discipline in nursing home and assisted living environments.

Good medicine first

The product idea is not to accelerate every case. It is to support better case selection habits, more complete documentation, and clearer follow-up visibility.

Designed to support

  • Appropriate-use thinking before Nova application
  • Objective ocular surface documentation
  • Prior measures and driver management
  • Pre-treatment photo capture or image import
  • Voice or text clinical notes
  • Follow-up reminders for mobile care settings

Prototype boundaries

  • Not production HIPAA infrastructure
  • Not billing or coding advice
  • Not a diagnostic image tool
  • Not a treatment approval engine

Nova is designed for ocular surface use by qualified healthcare professionals when clinically appropriate. Clinicians should follow the product IFU, payer policies, documentation requirements, and their own medical judgment.
